Okulotshiweyo

ï»¿Ukudlala uzungeze nezikhali zenuzi kumavidiyo kuyajabulisa.

Kukhona injabulo ye-visceral ekushayeni izinto,

nesifiso esibi ngezinto njengezicishamlilo, amashanguza nemisebe.

Futhi yize kusiza ukubeka okubhubhisayo amandla ngokubuka,

akuyona indlela engcono kakhulu yokuqonda umthelela wangempela wokuqhuma kwenuzi.

Lokhu akuphathelene nezinqwaba zamadolobha we-TNT, noma ukuthi kuqhume kangakanani ukuqhuma. Izikhali zenuzi zimayelana nani.

Ngakho-ke sihlanganyele neRed Cross kanye neRed Crescent ukunyakaza

ukubheka ukuthi bekuzokwenzekani ngempela uma ngabe kwenziwa isikhali senuzi edolobheni elikhulu namuhla.

Hhayi impi yenuzi, ukuqhuma okukodwa nje.

Siqala indaba yethu phakathi ezansi edolobheni elikhulu.

Abantu bazosebenza, bafundele izivivinyo, balahlekile emiqondweni yabo nasekuphileni kwansuku zonke.

Lapha, kufakwe isikhali senuzi nesikhathi siyabanda.

Isigaba sokuqala sokuqhuma senzeka kungakapheli umzuzwana.

Kwi-millisecond, ibhola le-plasma elishisa kakhulu kuneLanga liyavela futhi likhula ebhola lomlilo liye kumakhilomitha angaphezu kwama-2 ukuwela.

Kuleli bhola, wonke umuntu usehambile.

Cabanga ngamanzi adonselwe epanini elishisa kakhulu.

I-sizzle, bese-ke akukho lutho.

Iningi lezakhiwo, izimoto, izihlahla, imidwebo ebunjiwe nabantu …

… konke kukhishwe.

Okokuqala, i-flash: i-tsunami enkulu yokukhanya igeza phezu komuzi khona manjalo.

Uma kwenzeka ikhanda lakho likhombe kwicala lokuqhuma,

kukukhipha uyimpumputhe amahora ambalwa.

Ukushisa kwalokhu kukhanya kuveza ukushaya ngamandla,

inamandla futhi iyashisa kangangokuba ivele ishise yonke into ibanga elingamakhilomitha ayi-13 ukusuka endaweni yokuqunjelwa phansi.

Lokhu kusho ukuthi yonke into esendaweni engamakhilomitha-skwele angama-500 okwaziyo ukushisa, iqala ukushiswa.

Ipulasitiki, ukhuni, indwangu, izinwele nesikhumba.

Uma kungenzeka ukuthi ufinyelele ekushayweni okushisayo, ngomzuzu owodwa, usendleleni yokusebenza,

umzuzu olandelayo, ususemlilo.

Manje isigaba sesibili siqala.

Kwenzeka ngemizuzwana embalwa.

Abantu abaningi manje bazoqala babone ukuthi kukhona okungahambi kahle,

kepha sekudlule isikhathi amakhulu ngamakhulu ezinkulungwane.

I-flash ilandelwa yi-shockwave.

Ukushisa nemisebe ye-fireball kudala ukuqhuma komoya omningi futhi ocindezelwe kakhulu ozungezile

lokho manje kukhula ngokuqhuma.

Kushesha kunejubane lomsindo,

ukudala imimoya enamandla kuneziphepho neziphepho.

Ingqalasizinda yabantu ayifani namandla ayo.

Izakhiwo ezinkulu kakhulu eziqhele ngamakhilomitha e-fireball zimane nje zazehlela ezansi izisekelo zazo.

Kukhonkolo qinisa insimbi kuphela okwaziyo Melana nengcindezi.

Kumapaki azungezile lapho umhlalaphansi ondla khona amadada,

izihlahla zimnyama futhi zajuluka kusukela ekushiseni umzuzwana ngaphambi kokuxubha njengamazinyo.

Uma ungaphandle, uyaphanjukiswa njengotshani bothuli esiwombeni esikhulu.

I-shockwave iba buthaka njengoba ihamba iya ngaphandle

kodwa noma kunjalo, cishe amakhilomitha-skwele ayi-175 ezindlu ewa sengathi enziwe ngamakhadi,

ukulandela amashumi ezinkulungwane zabantu owayengenasikhathi sokuphendula.

Kuqhuma iziteshi zikagesi nokusabalala komlilo kuyo yonke imfucumfucu.

Ifu lamakhowe elenziwe ngezinsalela ze-fireball, uthuli nomlotha

ikhuphuka amakhilomitha isibhakabhaka esibhakabhakeni emizuzwini embalwa elandelayo bese ibeka isithunzi esimnyama phezu kwedolobha elichithekile.

Lokhu kudonsela ngamandla umoya omusha ozungeze idolobha,

ukucekela phansi izakhiwo eziningi nokunikezela ubuningi be-oxygen.

Kuya ngedolobha ukuthi kwenzekani ngokulandelayo.

Uma kukhona uphethiloli owanele, imililo ingaphenduka isiphepho esivutha umlilo, wonke umuntu obhajwe kuwo

nabantu abazama ukubalekela incithakalo.

Kufika kumakhilomitha angama-21 ukusuka kulokhu kuqhuma, abantu abanjengawe bagijimela emawindini abo bezothatha izithombe zefu lamakhowe,

engazi ukuthi uthukile usezofika kubo,

cishe ukuqeda amafasitela abo futhi bakhe blizzard yengilazi ebukhali.

Isigaba sesithathu siqala emahoreni nasezinsukwini ezizayo.

Sijwayele umqondo wokuthi usizo luzofika, akunandaba inhlekelele.

Lesi sikhathi sehlukile: ukuqhuma kwenyukliya kufana nayo yonke inhlekelele yemvelo ngasikhathi sinye.

Kunamakhulu ezinkulungwane noma izigidi zabantu abalimele kakhulu:

ama-lacerations, amathambo aphukile, ukushiswa okukhulu.

Emizuzwini nase amahora ambalwa alandelayo, izinkulungwane ezengeziwe zizofa ngenxa yalokhu kulimala.

Abantu abaningi babhajwe ezakhiweni eziwohlokile njengasezamazama komhlaba noma baphuphuthekiswe ukukhanya.

isithulu ngenxa yokuqhuma kwegagasi futhi asikwazi ukubaleka emigwaqweni engenakutholakala ngemfucumfucu nemfucumfucu.

Bethukile, badidekile, futhi abazi ukuthi kwenzekeni kubo noma ngani.

Ngokunokwenzeka, izibhedlela eziningi zidalulwe kanye nazo zonke ezinye izakhiwo

futhi iningi labasebenza kwezokwelapha lifile noma lilimele,

kanye nawo wonke umuntu.

Abasindile baba nenhlanhla ngokwanele yokuba semakhubeni kamasipala noma bemi endaweni efanele ukungashiswa futhi kungashisi

ngeke ngikubalekele ukulimala okwamanje.

Kuya ngohlobo lwesikhali, lapho kuqhuma khona kanye nesimo sezulu,

kungaqala imvula enkulu emnyama,

ngomlotha we-radioactive nothuli lwehlela phezu komuzi, kumboza konke nawo wonke umuntu.

Into engabonakali, enonya, ethusayo ka- nemisebe iyashintsha.

Njalo ukuphefumula kuthwala ubuthi emaphashini abasindile.

Ezinsukwini ezizayo, abantu abathola imithamo ephezulu kakhulu yokuvezwa kwemisebe bazokufa.

Ngeke lube nosizo, hhayi amahora noma mhlawumbe izinsuku.

Impucuko ayisebenzi uma kukhona ukuwohloka okuphelele kwengqalasizinda.

Imigwaqo ivinjiwe, amathrekhi esitimela asontekile, imigwaqo egijimisana nodoti.

Akunamanzi, akukho ugesi,

akukho ukuxhumana, akukho zitolo zokuphakela okutholakalayo kusuka.

Usizo oluvela emadolobheni azungezile luzoba nesikhathi esinzima ukungena endaweni yenhlekelele

futhi noma bengakwazi, ukungcola kwe-radioactive kuzokwenza kube yingozi ukusondela kakhulu.

Ngemuva kokuhlasela kwenyukliya, uwedwa.

Ngakho-ke, kancane kancane, abantu baphuma kumfucumfucu ngonyawo,

kungcoliswe ukuqothuka kwemisebe, kuphethe okuncane okungenzeka ukuthi bakushiyile.

Ziyahamba kancane, zisezinhlungwini, zihlukumezekile, futhi zonke zidinga ukudla, amanzi kanye nokwelashwa ngokushesha.

Futhi umonakalo owenziwe yisikhali se-nyukli awupheli lapho imililo ivutha futhi intuthu icima.

Izibhedlela emadolobheni angomakhelwane azihlomele kahle ngenhlekelele yalesi silinganiso

futhi ukhungatheke amashumi noma amakhulu ezinkulungwane zeziguli ezilimele kakhulu.

Emavikini, izinyanga kanye neminyaka ezayo,

abaningi balabo abasindile bazonikezwa umdlavuza onjenge-leukemia.

Isizathu sokuthi akekho uhulumeni ofuna ucabange ngakho konke lokhu yingoba akukho mpendulo ebucayi yabantu yokuqhuma kwenuzi.

Ayikho indlela yokusiza ngempela izisulu zokuhlaselwa kwenyukliya ngokushesha.

Lesi akusona isivunguvungu, umlilo wasendle noma ukuzamazama komhlaba noma ingozi yenuzi.

Konke lokhu kuyizinto ngasikhathi sinye, kodwa okubi kakhulu.

Akukho sizwe emhlabeni esizimisele ukubhekana naso.

Umhlaba uguqukile eminyakeni embalwa edlule, nabaholi bomhlaba futhi

ngokusobala nangokuthusela umphakathi obala ngezikhali zenuzi.

Ochwepheshe abaningi bacabanga ukuthi ingozi yesiteleka senuzi iphezulu kunangaphambili.

Ohulumeni batshela izakhamizi zabo ukuthi kuhle ukuthi sinezikhali zenuzi,

kodwa kubi lapho omunye umuntu ebathola.

Ukuthi ngandlela thile kusongela abanye ngokubhubhisa ngobuningi ukusigcina siphephile.

Kepha ingabe lokhu kukwenza uzizwe uphephile?

Kuthatha kuphela iqembu elincane labantu elinamandla okuya ngokuhlanya noma ukuvuvukala, ukuqonda okungahambi kahle noma ukuqonda okulula

ukuvula inhlekelele yezilinganiso ezingenakucatshangwa.

Ukuqhuma izinto kumavidiyo kuyajabulisa.

Ukuqhuma izinto empilweni yangempela, hhayi kangako.

Kukhona ikhambi!

Ukuqeda zonke izikhali zenuzi futhi ufunge ukuthi ngeke uphinde ubakhe.

Ngo-2017, cishe 2/3 wawo wonke amazwe omhlaba,

kusekelwa amakhulukhulu ezinhlangano zomphakathi kanye ne-International Red Cross kanye ne-Red Crescent ukunyakaza

bavumile ukwenqabela nokususa izikhali zenuzi.

Akukhulunywa ngokuthi ngubani onezikhali zenuzi nokuthi ubani ongenazo. Izikhali uqobo lwazo ziyinkinga.

Baziphatha kabi kakhulu futhi bayingozi kithi sonke.

Akunandaba ukuthi uvela kweliphi izwe, noma ngabe uzithola uluphi uhlangothi lwezepolitiki,

kudingeka sifune ukuthi zinyamalale unomphela.

Lokhu ngeke kwenzeke ngaphandle kwengcindezi.

Uma ufuna ukuba yingxenye yale ngcindezi, kunezinto wena uqobo ongazenza:

Vakashela i-ononukes.org ukuze ufunde kabanzi ngezikhali zenuzi nokuthi yini ongayenza ngazo.
